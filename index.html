I travelled to the nearest embassy of China. There, I claimed my second nationality. I can legally claim it because my great grandfather went to China during the second Sino-japanese war as part of a aid group. He was a doctor, and eventually fell in love with my great grandmother, the legitimate heir to the throne of the Ming Dinasty. Unfortunately, the embassy workers didn't believed me, so I returned home upset.
